Rules & Regulations

Finale:
1. The solo-singing competition includes two categories. CATEGORY-1: Indian (classical/semiclassical) and CATEGORY-2: includes western songs. Each category will have different judgment.
2. A single participant is eligible to participate in ONLY ONE category.
3. There are prelims for the solo-singing competition. The participants need to make a video and NOT an audio recording). The video has to be mailed to: karvaan.avirbhav@gmail.com. A YouTube link to the video can also be shared through mail along with these details.
4. For the finals, the time limit is 3 minutes, which INCLUDES the set-up time.
5. The songs in the prelims and the finals can be different, but must belong to the same category.
6. NO INSTRUMENTS shall be provided by the college.
7. Only one accompanist is allowed.
8. Karaoke is allowed.
9. The decisions of judges will be final and binding.
10. Every participant MUST carry their valid ID cards.

The Group Singing Event

Rules & Regulations

1. Only one entry allowed per college. The group leader is requested to register ONLY ONCE for the whole group.
2. The time limit for the competition is 10 minutes. Exceeding the limit will result into negative marking. Kindly note that the given time limit INCLUDES the time for the set up.
3. The song may belong to any genre. There are no limitations.
4. The maximum number of group members is 20, INCLUDING accompanists.
5. Playing pre-recorded tracks during the performance is strictly not allowed.

Battle of Bands




×

Rules & Regulations

1. There are no on-the-spot registrations.
2. Only one entry per college is allowed.
3. Maximum allowed members for a band is 10.
4. At the time of registration, all the bands must send their performances recorded as videos as a part of the screening round (prelims) to be held. The videos must be of good quality and must be mailed to : karvaan.avirbhav@gmail.com. A YouTube link to the video can also be shared.
5. The mail must include: Name of the band, name of any one group-member and the college. (The ones sharing the YouTube link also need to send this mail along with these details.)
6. Since the videos are the sole criteria for selection, make sure that your best performance is sent to us.
7. After screening, 5 bands would be selected for the finals.
8. The max. time allowed is 30 minutes. Note that this time INCLUDES the stage set-up time.
9. The host college will be providing a drum set for the participating teams.
10. Cross-college teams are allowed.     

Battle on, Rockers!!
