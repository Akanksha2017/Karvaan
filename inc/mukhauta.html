The Street Play Event

Rules & Guidelines

Reporting time : 10:00am
Registrations can be done till 9:30am on the day of the event.However, the first 15 teams will be given the opportunity to perform.

On the day of the event, teams are required to submit :
-ID cards of the performing members
-Sources used in the process of play making
-List of performing members
Failure to submit the same will result in disqualification.

Rules
Team Limit : 25members       Time. Limit : 20+2 minutes
(Deduction of marks on exceeding the time limit)
*No limit on the props, musical instruments and choice of topic. However, a socially relevant topic would be appreciated.
*Obscenity or vulgarity of any sort will result in disqualification.
*The decision of the judge will be final and biding.

Battle on, Rockers!!




       For the celebration of views with a point, Mukhauta presents LALKAAR’16. Lalkaar is the annual street play event of Shaheed Rajguru College.It gives a stage of opportunities to voice opinions and bring to light issues that are lost in the daily circus of life.
Also, it gives to theatre enthusiasts, a space for creation, being and art.
The time is limited, the magic isn't.

FAQs

*Is it necessary for the entire to be present at the time of registration?
  -No, a single representative can do so. However, the team should be present by 10:00am.

*What will be the procedure for slot allotment?
 -Slots will be given as per the team's preference on first come first serve basis.

*Can our team, xyz exchange the given slot due to our performance in abc college ?
 -Yes, you may. However, this exchange should be done by the mutual consent of team representatives involved, with no help from the coordinators.

Rules & Regulations

*Time limit : 8-10mins
*No limit on usage of props, costumes etc.
*The college will not provide with any requirements including props, costumes etc.

*Decision of Judges shall be final and binding

REGISTRATION DETAILS:
*Registration will be on the spot and will be open until 30minutes prior to the competition.
*Individual or group participation will be entertained.
*Participation can be inter college or intra college, in case of group entries.
