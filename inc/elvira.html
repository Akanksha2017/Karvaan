       Elvira-the Fine Arts Society of Shaheed Rajguru College of Applied Sciences for Women is back this year with 'VISTOSO', a blend of exciting events that will surely challenge your creativity and imagination.

Cardina

The Card Making Competition


x

Rules & Regulations

1) Individual participation is allowed
2) Maximum time allotted for completion is 1 hour.
3) Participants are requested to bring their own material.
4) Two card sizes are allowed (A3 & A2).
5) Card has to be prepared at the designated venue only. Anything prepared in prior will not be accepted.
6) Crafting is also allowed and has to be done on the spot.




We believe that nothing is as unique, personal or well-received as something that's been lovingly handmade. And what's better than handmade cards. That's why, Elvira brings 'Cardina'-The Card Making competition at Karvaan 2016 for the creative card designers. Here's a chance to showcase your creativity by pulling together unbelievably fun and creative ideas and design your own card.

Inkfiesta

The Tattoo Making Competition


x

Rules & Regulations

1) A team of maximum 2 participants is allowed.
2) Participants are required to carry their own materials( like sketch pens, markers, acrylic paint etc.)
3) Time limit is 1 hour.
4) Tattoo must be based on the theme : Fantasy
5) Participants are not allowed to carry any tattoo design beforehand.
6) Tattoo can be made on arms or legs. Anything vulgar will not be accepted.






Tattoos are the ultimate form of personal expression. So why not design it yourself and make it truly unique? Inkfiesta is the tattoo artists' arena at Karvaan 2016.So gear up, sharpen your pen and staring drawing up some magic. It's time to spread the ink.

Fedoranza

The Headgear Designing Competition


x

Rules & Regulations

1.A team of 2 members is allowed.
2. Time limit would be 1.5 hrs.
3. Participants are required to bring their own materials.
4. Participants will be given a PROP after 40min from the commencement of the event. That prop must be incorporated in the head gear.
5. Head Gear should be in context with the theme : Fantasy.

Karvaan is just round the corner and we want to celebrate in style. Talking of style, what could be more innovative than a head gear!
