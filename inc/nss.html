NSS

       National Service Scheme popularly known as NSS Society of Shaheed Rajguru College Of Applied Sciences was started in 2014. First council of Rajguru College was formed in September'15.
Society comprises of many active and hard working members. With the team work, the society has organised many events in the college. Some of the activities of NSS Society are: Blood Donation Camp, Plantation Drive, Literacy Drive , Vermi-Composting, Eye Checkup Camp, Self Defence Program etc. The society has organized many competitions too for the college students like: Rangoli making, poster making, slogan writing, article writing etc. NSS volunteers have attended many events in different colleges too.
