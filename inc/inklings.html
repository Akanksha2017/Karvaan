Antithesis

The In-House Debate


×

Rules & Regulations

1. The team must consist of 2 members (cross teams allowed).
2. One of the two must speak for the motion and the other, against the motion.
3. The time limit is 4 minutes (3+1) for each speaker.
4. One interjection per speaker is allowed. No cross questioning is allowed.
5. The interjector must confine oneself to questions and not make statements, comments, or ask rhetorical questions.
6. The decision of the judges is subject to no intervention.

Battle on, Rockers!!

       Scrabble is a Word Game within which the player has to score points by placing words, each bearing a single letter, onto the gameboard. The words formed must be in crossword fashion, flow left to right in rows or downwards in columns. The words must be defined in a standard dictionary. So, get into the pool of this Classic Word Game, and scrabble on !!

Continuum Fantasia

JUST A MINUTE!


×

Rules & Regulations

1. Solo participation.
2. The participants will be given a random topic on which he/she has to speak for 60 seconds.
3. Repetition of the words/phrases should be avoided.
4. No deviation from the topic.
The person who wants to interrupt will have to hit the table and say the word JAM, explain the reason for interrupting, if the reason for jamming is correct according to the organizers, the person can speak for the remaining time.
5. No use of foul language.
6. Any contestant who does not abide by the guidelines of the game can be disqualified.
7. Decision of organizers will be final and binding.

!!




      Jam is a literary event in which the participants have to speak for one whole minute without hesitation, repetition and deviation. Well if you can, you've crossed the sea but if you can't then others can always interrupt and continue by saying 'just a minute'!
