Shuffleshots



       Photography is the language that can be understood everywhere in the world. Shuffleshots, the photography society of Shaheed Rajguru College was started in the year 2014 by the students to give college students an opportunity to express their photography talent in and outside college.

This year the society is back with its two events Lensarc, on field photography competition and Shootart, online photography competition. Register yourselves now and let your true colors shine through.

Lensarc


x

Rules & Regulations

i)Participants have to capture photographs from 10:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. and submit their THREE best clicks to our team(carry your own USB cable).
ii) Digital cameras with minimum 12MP, mobile phones with minimum 8MP and DSLRs are allowed.
iii)No editing of the photographs is allowed. Only minor color corrections and cropping is allowed.

For any queries email us at shuffleshot.srg@gmail.com
