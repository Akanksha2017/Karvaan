Rules & Regulations

1. One team per college of maximum 18 members including the backstage helpers.
2. Time limit 10-12 minutes including narrative time. Team exceeding the time limit will be disqualified.
3. Choreography should be theme based.

      Dance is frequently described as being “about” movement. “Dance,” writes Ann Daly, “although it has a visual component, is fundamentally a kinesthetic art”. Audience experiences of dance can therefore be conceptualized in terms of responses to movement, most prominently in terms of what has been described as “kinesthetic empathy.
    Even with the eyes closed, one is aware of the positions of his legs and arms and can perceive the movement of a limb and its direction. The term kinesthesis has been coined for this sensibility.
