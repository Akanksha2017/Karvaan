Rules & Guidelines

GENERAL GUIDELINES
1) All the participants of a team are required to bring their college identity cards. Each student in the team should be a registered student of that college.
2) Only one entry per college is allowed.
3) Round 1: Prelims
Send your photographs to vogue.karvaan16@gmail.com latest by 6th March 2016. Selected teams will be given a confirmation mail by 8th March 2016.(Dates are tentative)
Round 2: Finale
10th March 2016(Tentative)
4) Green room will be provided for changing purpose.
5) Participants have to carry their own costumes, make-up, props, materials used in the show.
6) Participants have to carry their music in pen drive and also carry a backup.
7) Teams need to report at the registration desk latest by 9:30 am.
8) Decision of judges will be final & binding. The college reserves the right, in its sole discretion, to disqualify any entry at any time in the event if it is determined that the entrant has not compiled with the rules.

GENERAL RULES
1) Time limit: Minimum 5 min and Maximum 10 min inclusive of set up time.
2) Maximum number of participants allowed are 15 including coordinators, make-up artists and designers.
3) Participants will be judged on theme, apparels, coordination, theme portrayal, X-factor.
4) No hazardous props are allowed.
5) Penalty on exceeding the time limit.
6) Disqualification on not abiding by the member rule.
